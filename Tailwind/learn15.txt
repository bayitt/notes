Whitspace
These are utilities for controlling an element's whitespace property

.whitespace-normal, .whitespace-no-wrap, .whitespace-pre, .whitespace-pre-line, .whitespace-pre-wrap

Use .whitespace-normal to cause text to wrap normally within an element. Newlines and spaces will be collapsed.
.whitespace-no-wrap to prevent text from wrapping within an element. Newlines and spaces will be collapsed.
.whitespace-pre to prevent newlines and spaces from being collapsed. Text will not be wrapped.

Utilities for controlling word breaks in elements
.break-normal, .break-words, .break-all, .truncate
