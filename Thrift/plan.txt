What exactly do I want to do?

An expense tracking application

Features
- Expense Tracking
- At the beginning of every month, you enter your income for the month, this amount can be increased but not decreased, you also enter an amount to be saved,
again, this amount can be increased but not decreased
- There is a timer counting down to the end of the month
- Users can view the top three items of all time they spend on
- They can also view their projected spending for the month and if they end up with a surplus or deficit
- They can search for a particular item and view everytime they spent on it
- They can analyze their spending via graphical means
- They can compare spending between two months
- They can also generate PDF spending reports for particular periods
