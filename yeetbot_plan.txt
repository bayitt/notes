Yeetbot plan
Accompanying bot for the Yeet app. Written in Python via the python-telegram-bot library. 
What should the bot be able to do ?
- Enable users to set their telegram IDs (authenticate)
- Display a list of all stocks and cryptocurrencies they are subscribed to
- Display a list of stocks users are subscribed to
- Display a list of cryptocurrencies users are subscribed to
- Message users every night at 8:00PM with stock and trade price roundups
- Update their Telegram IDs
